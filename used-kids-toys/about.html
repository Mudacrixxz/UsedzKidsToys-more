About Us

Our Story

Used Kids Toys and More was founded in 2020 by a group of parents who were passionate about sustainable living and providing affordable toys for children. What started as a small local exchange program has grown into an online marketplace dedicated to giving pre-loved toys a second life.

We believe that quality toys shouldn't cost a fortune, and that by reusing and recycling toys, we can make a positive impact on both families' budgets and the environment.

Our Mission

At Used Kids Toys and More, our mission is to:

  • Provide families with access to high-quality, affordable toys
  • Reduce toy waste by extending the lifespan of well-made toys
  • Create a community of like-minded parents who value sustainability
  • Offer exceptional customer service and a seamless shopping experience

Quality Promise

Every toy that comes through our doors undergoes a thorough inspection and cleaning process. We check for:

  • Safety and structural integrity
  • Completeness (all parts present)
  • Cleanliness and hygienic condition
  • Proper functionality

If a toy doesn't meet our standards, it doesn't make it to our shelves. It's as simple as that.

Environmental Impact

Since our founding, we've helped find new homes for over 50,000 toys, preventing approximately 25 tons of plastic from entering landfills. Our packaging is made from recycled materials, and we continually look for ways to reduce our carbon footprint.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you ensure toy safety?

Every toy undergoes a thorough safety inspection, including checking for loose parts, sharp edges, and proper functionality. We follow strict safety guidelines and only accept toys that meet our high standards.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all purchases. If you're not completely satisfied, you can return the item for a full refund or exchange.

How do you clean the toys?

All toys are professionally cleaned and sanitized using child-safe cleaning products. We follow a detailed cleaning protocol to ensure every toy is hygienic and ready for its new home.
