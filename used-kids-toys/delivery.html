Delivery Areas

We currently ship to all addresses within the United Kingdom, including Northern Ireland, Scottish Highlands, and other remote areas. For international shipping, please contact our customer service for a quote.

Tracking Your Order

Once your order has been dispatched, you will receive an email with tracking information. You can also view the status of your order by logging into your account and visiting the "Order History" section.

Delivery Times

Please note that all delivery times are estimates and are calculated in working days (Monday to Friday, excluding public holidays). While we strive to meet these delivery estimates, occasional delays may occur due to:

  • High order volumes during busy periods
  • Adverse weather conditions
  • Courier service disruptions
  • Remote delivery locations

We will inform you of any significant delays to your delivery.

Packaging

At Used Kids Toys and More, we are committed to sustainability. All our products are packaged using recycled or recyclable materials. Our packaging is designed to ensure that your toys arrive in perfect condition while minimizing environmental impact.

Delivery Instructions

You can provide specific delivery instructions during the checkout process. These might include:

  • Safe place for delivery if you're not home
  • Preferred delivery time window
  • Special access instructions

Our delivery partners will do their best to follow these instructions, but cannot guarantee that all requests will be accommodated.

Failed Deliveries

If no one is available to receive your delivery, our courier will:

  1. Attempt to leave your package in a safe place (if you've specified one)
  2. Leave a card with instructions on how to arrange redelivery
  3. Take your package to a local collection point (where available)

After three failed delivery attempts, your package may be returned to us. In this case, you will be contacted to arrange redelivery, which may incur additional charges.
