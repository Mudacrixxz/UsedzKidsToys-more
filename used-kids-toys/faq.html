Frequently Asked Questions

How can we help you?

How do I place an order?

To place an order, simply browse our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to provide shipping details and payment information to complete your purchase. Once your order is confirmed, you'll receive an order confirmation email.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept the following payment methods:

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
  • PayPal
  • Apple Pay

All transactions are secure and encrypted for your protection.

Can I modify or cancel my order after it's placed?

You can modify or cancel your order within 1 hour of placing it, as long as it hasn't been processed for shipping. Please contact our customer service team as soon as possible at orders@usedkidstoys.com or call 0123 456 789.

How long will it take to receive my order?

Standard delivery typically takes 3-5 working days within the UK. Express delivery is available for 1-2 working days, and Next Day delivery is available for orders placed before 2pm (Monday-Friday, excluding holidays).

You can find more detailed information on our Delivery Information page.

Do you offer free shipping?

Yes, we offer free standard shipping on all orders over £50. For orders under this amount, standard shipping costs £3.99.

How can I track my order?

Once your order has been dispatched, you'll receive an email with tracking information. You can also view the status of your order by logging into your account and visiting the "Order History" section.

What is your returns policy?

You can return any item within 14 days of receiving your order for a full refund or exchange, provided the item meets our return conditions. Please see our Returns Policy page for full details.

How do I return an item?

To initiate a return:

  1. Contact our customer service team at returns@usedkidstoys.com or call 0123 456 789 within 14 days of receiving your order.
  2. Provide your order number and details of the item(s) you wish to return, along with the reason.
  3. Our team will review your request and provide a Return Authorization Number and instructions.
  4. Package the item securely with all parts and accessories.
  5. Send the package to the address provided in the return instructions.

When will I receive my refund?

Once we receive and inspect the returned item(s), your refund will be processed within 3-5 business days. The refund will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase. Depending on your bank or credit card company, it may take an additional 5-10 business days for the refund to show on your account.

How do you determine the condition of toys?

We have a rigorous inspection process for all toys. Our condition ratings are:

  • Like New: Appears unused, complete with all parts and original packaging.
  • Excellent: Minimal signs of use, all parts present, fully functional.
  • Very Good: Some signs of previous use, minor cosmetic marks, all essential parts present.
  • Good: Visible signs of use, may have minor defects that don't affect functionality.

Each product listing includes detailed information about the condition of the specific item.

Are your toys cleaned and sanitized?

Yes, all toys undergo a thorough cleaning and sanitization process before being listed for sale. Our cleaning procedures vary based on the toy material but always include disinfection with child-safe products. Plush toys are cleaned using heat treatment methods, and plastic toys are cleaned with non-toxic disinfectants.

Do toys come with original packaging?

It depends on the specific toy. Some items do come with their original packaging, while others may not. The product description will always specify whether original packaging is included. If original packaging is important to you, please check the product details carefully before purchasing.

Do I need to create an account to make a purchase?

While creating an account offers benefits like order tracking, saved addresses, and wishlist functionality, we also offer a guest checkout option. You can make purchases without creating an account, but you'll need to provide your shipping and payment details for each order.

How is my personal information handled?

We take your privacy seriously and comply with all applicable data protection regulations. Your personal information is used solely for processing orders, improving your shopping experience, and communicating with you about your purchases. We never sell your data to third parties.

For complete details, please review our Privacy Policy.

How can I subscribe to or unsubscribe from your newsletter?

You can subscribe to our newsletter by entering your email in the subscription box on our homepage. To unsubscribe, simply click the "Unsubscribe" link at the bottom of any newsletter email we send you, or contact our customer service team at info@usedkidstoys.com.
