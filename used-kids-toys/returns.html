Returns Policy

Our Return Promise

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase from Used Kids Toys and More. Though all our toys are pre-owned, we maintain strict quality standards and thoroughly check each item before it's listed for sale. However, we understand that sometimes a purchase may not meet your expectations.

Return Period

You can return any item within 14 days of receiving your order for a full refund or exchange, provided the item meets our return conditions.

Return Conditions

To be eligible for a return, your item must be:

  • In the same condition as you received it
  • Complete with all parts and accessories
  • In its original packaging (where applicable)
  • Unused (except to check functionality)
  • Free from additional damage or marks beyond what was described in the listing

Non-Returnable Items

For hygiene reasons and consumer protection, the following items cannot be returned:

  • Stuffed toys that have been used
  • Items with damaged seals (e.g., board games with plastic wrap removed)
  • Bath toys that have been used
  • Personalized items
  • Items marked as "final sale" or "as-is" in the product description

Return Process

To initiate a return, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our customer service team at returns@usedkidstoys.com or call 0123 456 789 within 14 days of receiving your order.
  2. Provide your order number and details of the item(s) you wish to return, along with the reason for your return.
  3. Our team will review your request and provide a Return Authorization Number and instructions for returning the item.
  4. Package the item securely with all parts and accessories.
  5. Include the Return Authorization Number on your package.
  6. Send the package to the address provided in the return instructions.

Return Shipping

For returns due to:

  • Damaged or faulty items: We will cover the cost of return shipping. We may arrange for collection of the item or provide a prepaid shipping label.
  • Incorrect items sent: We will cover the cost of return shipping.
  • Change of mind: The customer is responsible for return shipping costs.

Refunds

Once we receive and inspect the returned item(s), we will notify you about the status of your refund:

  • If approved, your refund will be processed within 3-5 business days.
  • The refund will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase.
  • Depending on your bank or credit card company, it may take an additional 5-10 business days for the refund to show on your account.

Exchanges

If you wish to exchange an item for a different product, please follow the return process and mention in your message that you would like an exchange. Once your return is approved, you can place a new order for the desired item, or we can assist you with the exchange if the item is still available.

Damaged Items

If you receive a damaged item:

  1. Take photographs of the damage.
  2. Contact us within 48 hours of receiving the delivery.
  3. Include your order number and photos of the damaged item.
  4. Our team will investigate and offer a suitable solution (replacement, repair, or refund).

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our returns policy, please contact our customer service team:

  • Email: returns@usedkidstoys.com
  • Phone: 0123 456 789 (9am-5pm, Monday to Friday)
